Impossible Bulgogi

Description

Omsom's super tasty and traditional bulgogi starter sauce is a flavorful mix of gochujang,
garlic, onion, soy sauce, ginger, sugar sesame oil, garlic, onion and here's the kicker:
pear juice. Is your mouth watering yet? Ours too. Let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 12 Ounce Package Impossible Burger
  • 1/2 Small Sweet Onion
  • 3 Tablespoons Neutral Cooking Oil
  • 1-1.8 Ounce Omsom Korean Spicy Bulgogi Starter Pack
  • 1 Scallion, Sliced
  • Toasted Sesame Seeds To Taste
  • Optional: Serve With Lettuce Wraps, White Rice And Kimchi, Or Over A Lettuce, Cucumber,
    And Perilla Salad

Steps

  1. Thinly slice half of a small, sweet onion. On medium high heat, heat 1 tablespoon
    cooking oil in a skillet for 1 minute. Add onion and cook for 3 minutes until soft. Then
    remove and set aside.

  2. Add an additional 2 tbsp oil to skillet. Add Impossible Burger, break up into small
    crumbles, and saute until there is no more pink.

  3. Add onions back in and mix. Mix in the Omson Korean Spicy Bulgogi Starter Pack and
    cook for an additional 2 minutes or until Impossible Burger is golden brown.

  4. Salt to taste, top with scallions and sesame seeds, and you're done! *Optional: Serve
    traditionally with lettuce wraps or white rice and kimchi, or mix it up and serve over a
    lettuce, cucumber, and perilla salad. Enjoy!
